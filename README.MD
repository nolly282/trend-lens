# Trend Lens - Systematic Trading Strategy

## What Is This?

Trend Lens is an algorithmic trading strategy built from scratch through a 
structured research process. It automatically identifies and executes trading 
opportunities across 8 financial instruments simultaneously, covering major 
cryptocurrencies and forex pairs.

The strategy was developed methodically, moving through five stages.:

1. **Hypothesis** - defining the potential edge before writing any code
2. **Code Validation** - building on one year of data and inspecting every signal manually
3. **Year-by-Year Testing** - validating performance across individual yearly slices
4. **Condition Analysis** - identifying where the strategy works and where it breaks down
5. **Walk-Forward Validation** - confirming that live results match the research

This repository documents that process and presents the results transparently.

---

## Why Does This Matter?

Most trading strategies that circulate online fall into one of three categories:

- Backtested with flawed methodology that accidentally looks into the future
- Validated on a single instrument or a very short time period
- Never actually deployed in live markets

Trend Lens was built differently. Every design decision, from how market conditions 
are classified to how trades are entered and exited, was made to reflect how the 
strategy would actually perform in real trading conditions, not just on a spreadsheet.

- The strategy has been profitable on every single instrument tested

## How to Read the Results

R multiples are used throughout this report rather than fixed dollar amounts. 
This is intentional. It makes the results honest and applicable to any account size.

1R is the amount risked on a single trade. If a trade wins, it returns 1.5R. 
If it loses, it costs 1R. The cumulative R across the portfolio tells you how 
many times your per-trade risk you would have earned over the period, regardless 
of how much that risk actually is in dollar terms.

You decide what 1R means for your account. The math scales with it.
